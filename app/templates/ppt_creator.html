Quick Start

How to Use

  1. 1
    Upload your files Drag & drop or click to add PDF, PNG, or JPG files (max 10 MB total).
  2. 2
    Enter your name Your name will appear on the generated presentation.
  3. 3
    Add instructions (optional) Guide the AI — e.g. "Focus on key findings" or "Create exactly 8 slides".
  4. 4
    Generate & download Click Generate Presentation, then download the ready-made .pptx file.
  5. 5
    Refine with chat After generation, use the Refine Content panel to ask the AI to adjust any slides.

💡 Tips

  • Text-based PDFs produce the best results — scanned/image PDFs may lose content.
  • Image files (PNG/JPG) are read directly by the AI using vision — great for charts or screenshots.
  • Be specific in your instructions to get a more focused presentation structure.
