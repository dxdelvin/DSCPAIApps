Step 1

Confluence Connection

Set your Docupedia/Confluence target and authenticate with a Personal Access Token before uploading content.

How to get your Personal Access Token
  1. Open your Docupedia or Confluence instance and go to Profile → Settings → Personal Access Tokens.
  2. Click Create token, give it a name (e.g. "DSCP Publisher"), and copy the generated value.
  3. Paste the token below. It is only sent with your publish request and is never stored.
